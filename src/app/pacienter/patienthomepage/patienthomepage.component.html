*PLATANOS, LIMÓN VERDE (9.9 pH)

*LIMÓN AMARILLO (8.2 pH)

*AGUACATE (15.6 pH)

*AJO (13.2 pH)

*MANGO (pH 8.7)

*MANDARINA (8.5 pH)
